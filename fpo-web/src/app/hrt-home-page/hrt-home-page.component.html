How to use this form

  • Complete this form to file discrimination complaint in BC for yourself or another person
  • Do not attach evidence about your complaint, unless it is an employment ad or publication you say is discriminatory. The Tribunal will tell you when you need to submit evidence to support your complaint.
  • If you are filing the complaint for another person, you must also file a Form 1.2 Authorization (unless you are their lawyer or legal advocate). Get the Form 1.2 on the Tribunal’s website

1-year time limit to make complaint

  • Submit this form within 1 year of the discrimination, if possible.
  • If you file late, you can ask the Tribunal to accept your complaint when you fill out this form.

Do you need help?

  • We recommend you get legal advice about your complaint before submitting it, if possible.
  • See Who Can Help? on the Tribunal’s website

What will the Tribunal do with this form?

The Tribunal will read the form to see if you set our possible discrimination under the Human Rights Code.

  • The Tribunal will usually ask you for more information.
  • If you set out possible discrimination, the Tribunal will give a copy of your complaint to Respondents so they can respond to your complaint.

Some things to know about the online complaints form

  • An * on the form means you must answer this question
  • You can use this form on your computer or on your smartphone
  • This form does not work with Internet Explorer 8 or Compatibility Mode
  • For more information see this website

You have two options to fill out the form

  • Continue as Guest.

    • This option is easiest.
    • This option won’t save your work if you close the form or if you leave the form for more than 30 minutes.
    • This option gives you 8 hours to complete the form. But you can’t leave the form for more than 30 minutes.

  • Login to Continue.

    • This option requires you to create an account. You can choose a Basic account.
    • After you create an account, click “Continue to Service Directory” and search “HRT”.
    • In your form, you can click “Save for Later”. You can login later to work on your form.
